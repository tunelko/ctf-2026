## Combinación de pistas

## Factorización
- p = 2^777 - 85839 (777 bits)
- q = n / p (777 bits)
- n = 1554 bits total

## Descifrado RSA
- phi = (p-1)(q-1), d = e^-1 mod phi
- m = c^d mod n → "S3cr3t_to_p4pis_f0rtune"
